The Eight Key Roles in Big Data

A collaborative view of the critical players driving data success.

Data Ops

Ensures the reliability, quality, and automated delivery of data pipelines, bridging the gap between development and operations.

Data Engineer

Designs, builds, and maintains the large-scale data processing systems and databases required for analytics and ML.

ML Engineer

Focuses on deploying and maintaining machine learning models in production environments, ensuring scalability and performance.

Data Scientist

Analyzes complex datasets to extract insights, create predictive models, and guide strategic decision-making.

Apps Developer

Builds user-facing applications that consume the insights and processed data delivered by the data platform.

Administrator

Manages and maintains the infrastructure, security, and access controls for the big data environment and related tools.

Architect

Defines the overall structure, technologies, and standards for the entire big data ecosystem to meet business needs.

Data Governance

Establishes policies and procedures for data handling, compliance, quality, and ethical use across the organization.
